Introducing the blog

2018-03-28

Introduction


Hi I’m Kudakwashe Paradzayi and currently I am studying Software Engineering at the Harare Institute of Technology in Zimbabwe. I am introducing myself and this blog



I was introduced to programming in 2014 and ever since then I have been in love with programming. All these years I have gained some experience, some of which I will share in my next blogs.


I have programmed using several languages such as VB.net, c#, c/c++, Javascript, python and kotlin. Even though I know all these programming languages I have made a commitment to myself to lean more to expand my tool set. With the dynamic nature of our industry we ought to keep abreast with the technological changes so that we remain relevant.

Why am I blogging

I have 2 major reasons why I chose to start a blog

  • I have learned a great deal from other people’s blogs and this blog is my way of giving back to the community.
  • These days I am learning a lot from books by the likes of Martin Fowler, Robert Martin and Greg Young and I feel that I will consolidate that knowledge more if I share it with others.

What this blog will include

I will mostly write about software engineering concepts including but not limited to

  • Software architecture (microservices, cqrs, etc)
  • Methods of Software design (agile, extreme, etc)
  • Programming Paradigms
  • Test driven development/ behavior driven development
  • Domain driven development
  • Some of the cool latest tech in the programming space
  • Real world application development in tutorial form
  • HTTP/S
  • Web development
  • Mobile development
  • And many more concepts…

Most of the stuff here is highly theoretical but I will include many supporting practical examples to make it easy to understand.


I will be posting blog posts on my website (http://kudapara.co.zw) and on my medium account (https://medium.com/@kudapara). If you have any questions or suggestions you can give me a shout out on twitter (@kudapara)

My sincere request is that you to share these posts with your friends so that we impart the precious knowledge of Software to everyone.

Take home

Keep leaning and never give up
